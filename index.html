About Us

We are Scouts BSA Troop 72 out of Park City, Utah. We range in age from 11-17. Our adult leadership is Scoutmaster Eric Armentrout, and scout leadership roles are shown further down the page. Every meeting is fun and the Scouts are very nice. We meet every Tuesday from 6 to 7 PM and we have many campouts throughout the year. We also do a merit badge council every Sunday from 1 - 4 PM for those looking to complete their Eagle Scout required merit badges. If you are interested in joining our Troop, please contact Eric Armentrout (413) 320-6792.
