About Us

We are Scouts BSA Troop 72 out of Park City, Utah. We range in age from 11-17. Our adult leadership is Scoutmaster Mark Jankowske, and scout leadership roles are shown further down the page. Every meeting is fun and the Scouts are very nice.

We meet every Tuesday from 6 to 7 PM and we have a campout almost every month throughout the year. In the winter, we also do a merit badge council on Sundays from 1 - 4 PM for those looking to complete their Eagle Scout required merit badges. If you are interested in joining our Troop, please contact Mark Jankowske at (435) 659-06322.
